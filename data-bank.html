Data Bank (SQL)

SQL PostgreSQL MySQL Database Design Optimization NoSQL Redis ETL

In the modern era, data is the most valuable commodity. A slow, poorly structured database can cripple an application, no matter how beautiful the frontend might look. My expertise with SQL and relational data models ensures that information is securely stored, rapidly retrieved, and mathematically sound.

What I Do

I design highly normalized schemas to preserve data integrity and denormalize strategically when query performance becomes the priority. My tasks often involve writing complex multi-table joins, establishing sophisticated indexing patterns, writing stored procedures, and handling intricate data migrations. Beyond SQL databases like PostgreSQL and MySQL, I integrate caching mechanisms using Redis to minimize direct database stress on heavy read-loads.

My Approach

Security and scale are paramount. I enforce stringent access controls and parameterized querying to prevent injection attacks out-of-the-box. When planning a data architecture, I always anticipate future growth—ensuring indexing structures and sharding strategies are viable long before table sizes balloon into the millions of rows, preventing disastrous performance bottlenecks down the line.
