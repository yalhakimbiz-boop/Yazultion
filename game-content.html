Game Content

Video games are the ultimate intersection of technology and art. Creating compelling game content requires an understanding of player psychology, narrative pacing, and technical performance boundaries. I excel at bridging the gap between an imaginative concept and an executable reality.

What I Do

From gray-boxing initial layouts to final art passes, I create environments that tell a story. I design and implement core gameplay mechanics, fine-tuning character physics, AI behaviors, and item balancing. Working extensively with game engines such as Unity and Unreal Engine, I pipeline custom 3D assets and auditory effects to create highly immersive and cohesive worlds.

My Approach

Game design is highly iterative. I prioritize rapid prototyping—testing the "fun factor" before committing to extensive art assets. I believe that performance is a massive part of game feel; therefore, I am rigorous about polygon optimization, efficient texture mapping, and optimal garbage collection in code to ensure consistently high frame rates regardless of the platform.
